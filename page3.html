Customer Churn Analysis

This project features an interactive dashboard made in Power BI for analyzing the customer churn data of a ficticious company named Databel. The data was provided by DataCamp in one of their Power BI courses. The entire design of this dashboard was made using Power BI.

The dashboard contains:

  • Interactive card visuals that display the total churn rate, number of customers and numbers of churned customers.
  • Filters for contract category and payment method.
  • Graphs that display the churn reason, churn rate by gender, churn rate by state, churn rate by account length, and churn rate by age group.

Insights:

  • Overall Churn Rate
  • Total Number of Customers vs Churned Customers
  • Churn Rate by Contract Category and Payment Method
