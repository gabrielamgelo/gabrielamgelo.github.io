Superstore Sales Performance

In this project I used Power BI to create a interactive dashboard that reveals valueable insights on the sales performance of ficticious store named Superstore. The background, cards, and icons were designed using Figma.

The dashboard contains:

  • Cards displaying information about the current year's total sales, orders, customers, and the year-over-year percentage change.
  • A filter icon that, when active, displays a filter panel for segment, category, and region
  • Graphs that display monthly trends of sales, total sales by category, sales by each states, sales by subcategory, and a table containing the products that generated the most sales.

Insights:

  • 2018 was the year with the best performance overall, but 2017 had the highest growth compared to the previous year.
  • Sales increased from August to December
  • The category that had the most sales overall was Technology.
  • The state with the most sales is California.
  • The top 5 subcategories with the most sales are: phones, chairs, storages, tables, and binders.
  • The West region had the best overall performance
  • The consumer segment is the segment with the best overall performance
